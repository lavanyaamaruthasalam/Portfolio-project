# Maven Market Profitability Insights – Excel Dashboard
---

This Excel project provides a detailed analysis of Maven Market’s sales, returns, and profitability. 
The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decisions around revenue, profitability, and operational efficiency across products and customers.

---

## Dashboard Overview
**Top-Line Profitability Dashboard** (`top_line_profitability.png`)  
This view provides a high-level summary of business performance, including:
- Gross revenue and gross profit per product
- Average profit margin
- Return impact on revenue
- Product-level and customer-level insights

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly identify top-performing products, high-return impact items, and overall profitability patterns.

---

## Underperforming Products Analysis
**Underperforming Products** (`underperforming_products.png`)  
This analysis highlights products with:
- High revenue but lower profit margins due to returns
- Significant return impact affecting net revenue
- Revenue alone not reflecting true profitability

**Key insights:**
- Certain high-revenue products are less profitable because of high return rates
- Repeated returns significantly impact overall margin efficiency
- Helps prioritize interventions around pricing, quality, and return reduction

---

## Customer Insights
**Customer Segmentation Dashboard** (`customer_insights.png`)  
This section analyzes customer behavior:
- Top 5 customers by revenue
- Top 5 customers by quantity purchased
- Average order value per customer
- Repeat vs one-time buyers

**Key insights:**
- A small group of high-value customers contributes disproportionately to revenue
- Return behavior affects perceived profitability at customer level
- Customer segmentation supports targeted sales and retention strategies

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (with pivots and charts)
- Value-only formulas for optimized performance
- Pivot Tables for revenue, profit, and return analyses
- Conditional formatting for highlighting key insights

---

## Project Outcome
The dashboard allows business teams to:
- Move beyond revenue-only analysis and focus on profitability and operational efficiency
- Quickly identify high-return, low-margin products
- Make informed decisions around pricing, inventory, and returns management
- Evaluate customer contributions and prioritize high-impact segments

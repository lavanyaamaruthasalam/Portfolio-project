# Maven Market – Sales Performance Analysis (Power BI)

This Power BI project analyzes sales performance data for Maven Market to identify revenue trends, profitability patterns, and operational risks across product brands.

The dashboard focuses on answering key business questions such as:
- How is the business performing at a topline level?
- Which brands generate high revenue but show lower margin efficiency?
- Where do returns impact profitability despite strong sales?

---

## Dashboard Overview

### 1. Topline Performance Dashboard
![Topline Performance]
(Topline_Performance.png)

This view provides a high-level summary of business performance, including:
- Total Revenue, Profit, and Returns
- Weekly revenue trends
- Product and brand-level performance
- Regional filtering for focused analysis

This dashboard helps stakeholders quickly assess overall business health.

---

### 2. Underperforming Brands Analysis
![Underperforming Brands]
(Underperforming_Brands.png)

This analysis highlights brands that generate high revenue but show reduced profit margin efficiency due to higher return rates.

**Key Insight:**
- Some high-revenue brands have lower profit margins compared to mid-revenue brands
- Higher return rates directly impact margin efficiency
- Revenue alone does not indicate true business performance

This insight supports data-driven decisions around pricing, quality control, and return reduction strategies.
